# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in language, tone, and technical disagreement.
- Assume good faith and focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior.
- Share context, reproduction steps, and evidence when reporting problems.
- Respect privacy and never post someone else's credentials or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on identity or protected characteristics
- Publishing private information, credentials, or security-sensitive material
- Disruptive behavior that repeatedly derails technical discussion

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this
policy, and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project and
its contributors.

## Reporting

Use the repository hosting platform's private maintainer contact channel for
conduct issues when available. If no private contact method is published yet,
open a minimal issue asking for a private reporting path without including
sensitive details.
